You can get our highway delivery service with SkyF heavy drones(longest flying time and heaviest payload drones) (world record). These drones have auto ground system without ground control . So, loads are very safe . Our highway delivery service has two extra services,H-H service and car delivery service.

What is H-H service?

H-H service means home to home(from home of a city to a home of another).It is a service that includes a highway delivery and two city deliveries.

In this service,you will pay for highway delivery cost and one city delivery cost(It means one city delivery is free).It is a service only for 1-8 kg around 3km from control center.Above this km , you will pay extra price shown in in city delivery page.

Car Delivery Service

Car delivery is a delivery service which for above 8 kg load delivered by highway drone.It is a service from home to drone center (or) from drone center to home.If you buy both,it will be a car H-H delivery.

For one way,it will cost 500 MMK (up to 10kg),1000 MMK (10kg - 20kg) and 1500 MMK (above 20kg) 15 km from control center.Above this , +up to 5km = +200 MMK.
